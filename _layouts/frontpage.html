Research in our group spans levels of organisation - from subcellular entities to societies. We are interested in elucidating the associations and interactions that power the emergent complexity at multiple scales. Our focus is on eco-evolutionary processes within and between the valuable but amorphous organisation of hierarchies of organisation. We not only utilise the toolbox of theoretical biology but simultaneously endeavour to develop it further. With "Insight preceding application" (Max Planck), we then eventually deploy our knowledge into action via translational programmes.
